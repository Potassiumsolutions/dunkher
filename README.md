# DunkHer 🌊

A dunk-tank twist on the classic word-guessing game. Guess the hidden word one letter at a time — miss too many and someone takes the plunge!

**▶️ Play it here: https://potassiumsolutions.github.io/dunkher/**

## How to play

- A hidden word appears as blanks. Tap letters to guess.
- Each **wrong** guess reveals one more piece of the character perched over the dunk tank.
- Nine wrong guesses and they get dunked — solve the word first to win.

## Features

- **Two characters:** play **DunkHer** (👩) or switch to **DunkHim** (👨) — each with its own build-up over the tank.
- **5 offline categories**, hundreds of words each, with built-in definitions: Animals, Countries, Food & Drink, Science, and Around the House.
- **Online mode** pulls fresh words from the Datamuse API with definitions from the Free Dictionary API.
- **Difficulty & length:** Easy or Hard mode, plus an optional word-length filter.
- **4 background themes** (🎨) and background music with mute and volume controls.
- Full on-screen A–Z keyboard, mobile-friendly, works on phones and desktop.

## Install it on your phone

DunkHer runs in the browser and can be added to your home screen like an app.

- **Android/Chrome:** open the link, then ⋮ menu → *Add to Home screen*.
- **iOS/Safari:** tap Share → *Add to Home Screen*.

## Tech

- Single-file vanilla HTML / CSS / JavaScript, no build step
- SVG character art revealed piece-by-piece
- Datamuse + Free Dictionary APIs for online word mode
- Hosted on GitHub Pages (local files open in iOS Quick Look, which blocks the JavaScript — hosting over HTTPS fixes that)
